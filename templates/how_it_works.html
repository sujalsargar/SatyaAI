How SatyaAI Works

A step-by-step verification pipeline for text, URLs, images, audio, and video.

1. Ingest

Accepts text, article URLs, image uploads (OCR), audio files (speech-to-text) and video (audio extraction + frame OCR).

2. Extract

OCR (Tesseract) for images/frames, Whisper for speech-to-text, and HTML scraping for URLs.

3. Cross-check

Compare extracted claims with trusted sources like Wikipedia, Google News, Snopes, FactCheck, PolitiFact.

4. Reason

Ask the LLM (OpenAI/Gemini) to weigh evidence and provide a structured verdict with confidence and sources.

5. Return

Structured JSON result and a human-readable explanation, saved to your history for audit and review.
